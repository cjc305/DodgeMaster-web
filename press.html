DodgeMaster 是 Android 與 iOS 雙平台精準動作遊戲。玩家於 100 關手作關卡中以「點擊跳躍 / 長按減速」操控小球閃避各種陷阱。採 F2P + 飾品 IAP 模式，無強迫廣告、無付費跳關，設計理念是尊重玩家專注力。由台灣獨立工作室 Crealize 開發。

DodgeMaster is a precision platformer for Android and iOS. Players navigate 100 hand-crafted levels by tapping to jump and long-pressing to slow time. The game features a cosmetic-only DodgeCoin economy with zero forced ads, designed to respect player attention. Made by indie studio Crealize Studio in Taiwan.
